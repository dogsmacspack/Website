Meet

MAC

I am a seasoned professional with a deep passion for dogs and an extensive background in dog care. With 15 years of experience in the field and 5 years of professional dog care, I have honed my skills in doggy day care, drop-ins, walking, running, and overnight stays.

My expertise is backed by a degree in criminal justice from Metropolitan State University (MSU) Denver, where I also excelled as a soccer-scholarship athlete. Additionally, I have 5 years of law enforcement experience, showcasing my dedication to responsibility and safety.

Prior to establishing my own LLC, I have gained valuable experience working for renowned companies in the industry, including Rover, Wag, and Denver Dog Joggers. These experiences have helped shape my philosophy of providing a comfortable and home-like environment for your dog, ensuring they become an integral part of my pack rather than being confined to a kennel or left alone.

To guarantee the highest level of professionalism and peace of mind, I am fully insured. When your beloved companion joins my pack, they will have the opportunity to socialize and interact with my own dogs. My pack currently includes Nova, a vibrant 7-year-old Catahoula Leopard Dog, Nanook, a friendly 7-year-old Husky/Malamute mix, and Icess, a spry 16-year-old Husky.

I am committed to maintaining my own physical fitness, enabling me to meet the challenges of managing a "pack" and providing the utmost care for your dog during daycare, boarding, and dog park adventures.

I am excited about the opportunity to get to know you and your furry companions. Rest assured that with my extensive experience, genuine love for dogs, and dedication to their well-being, your pets will receive exceptional care under my watch.
